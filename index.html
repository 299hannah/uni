How To apply at zetech university

To gain admission for undergraduate, diploma or certificate training at Zetech University, you must have successfully completed a recognized secondary education qualification.

Step 1. Select your course or programme Zetech University offers a wide range of programmes. When choosing your course, there is a lot for you to consider such as; What type of course you should do, what qualification will you get and what is your desired career path. Take time and choose a course that suits you best.

Step 2. Check entry requirements Depending on the course you would like to apply for, check that you meet our entry requirements. If you do not meet these requirements, there are still options available for you.

Step 3. Check for credit eligibility If you have post secondary educational qualifications, you may be entitled to receive exemptions. Applicants seeking exemptions should include academic transcripts and curriculum details in their applications.

Step 4. Complete and submit an application form. If you meet all the above requirements, then you are ready to complete our application form. Admission forms may be obtained from the admissions office at our Thika Road campus or from any of our campuses across Nairobi. Application forms can also be downloaded from our website: zetech.ac.ke

When to apply The University runs three academic intakes in a year, in the following months; January, May and September.
All applicants must fill the form in duplicate and attach:
A recent passport size photos
A copy of KCSE result slip
A copy of your National ID
Completed application/registration forms MUST be submitted together with a copy of a bank deposit slip as follows:
Degree: Kshs. 1000
Diploma: Kshs. 500
Certificate: Kshs. 500
